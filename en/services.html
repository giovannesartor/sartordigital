Frequently Asked Questions about Services

Clear your doubts about our services, deadlines, values and processes.

How does the quote process work?

We conduct a free discovery meeting to understand your needs. Then, we prepare a detailed proposal with scope, schedule and investment. The quote is divided by phases, allowing greater control and flexibility.

What forms of payment are accepted?

We accept various forms: credit card installment (up to 12x), bank transfer, PIX and bank slip. For larger projects, we offer special conditions with payment by delivered phases.

Do you offer guarantee on services?

Yes, all our projects include 90 days guarantee for bug fixes and necessary adjustments. For development services, we also offer 30 days of free post-implementation support.

How does a lead capture system work?

We develop a complete ecosystem: 1. Conversion-optimized landing page | 2. Strategic value content (like ebooks or whitepapers) | 3. Intelligent capture form | 4. Email automation system | 5. Integration with your CRM | 6. Analytical dashboard with performance metrics. The system works 24/7 capturing and qualifying leads automatically.

Can we request changes during development?

Yes, we work with agile methodology that allows adjustments during development. Changes within the initial scope are included. For significant scope changes, we evaluate the impact on schedule and budget transparently.

Who will be the team responsible for my project?

Each project has a dedicated manager who will be your main point of contact. The technical team is formed by specialists in the technologies needed for your project. All have proven experience and undergo constant training.
