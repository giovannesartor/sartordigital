architecture

How I design a system

Not a "website" or a "hack that works in the demo". It's architecture designed to grow, be observed and survive traffic spikes — from the first commit.

Edge firstContent and cache at the edge (Cloudflare) for low latency anywhere in the world.
Guarded APIAuthentication, rate limiting and validation before any business rule touches the data.
Async workQueues, workers and background jobs so the system doesn't stall under load.
Resilient dataPostgreSQL as source of truth, Redis for cache and speed under load.
AI on your dataRAG and semantic search with a vector database — grounded answers, not guesswork.
ObservabilityLogs, metrics and alerts from day 1 — issues caught before the customer notices.

security & compliance

Security is not an add-on

For fintech, healthtech and agtech, data is a responsibility. I treat it that way from the first line.

Robust authentication

OAuth and SSO where applicable, secure sessions and least-privilege principle.

Protected data

Encryption in transit and at rest; secrets kept out of code, in a config vault.

Privacy by design

Compliance considered in the data model — consent, minimization and retention.

Resilience

Rate limiting, queues and cache to handle traffic spikes without bringing the system down.
